About Me

Emily Balden

I was born in Sydney, Australia and I moved about every two years. I did high school in Santa Rosa Beach Florida and I moved to BYU for school in the fall. I love the gospel and I love the world that God has given me. I am half-Vietnamese and just got called on a mission to Hanoi, Vietnam and I couldn't be more excited! I work at the BYU Store Sweet Shop and I love my job there but I will be leaving back home to Australia soon. I am pre-Global Supply Chain management and I am going to help make the fashion industry more sustainable and have a better impact one day.
